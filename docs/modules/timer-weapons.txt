Timer-Weapons

This plugins only for CS:Source.
It provides a weapon cleaner which allows to keep map spawned weapons.
A buyzone everywhere, but you have to extend mp_buytime to use this.
And some other features.

Commands:
sm_knife - Give player a knife");
sm_scout - Give player a scout");
sm_usp - Give player a usp");
sm_glock - Give player a glock");
